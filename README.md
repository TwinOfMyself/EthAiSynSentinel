# EthAISyn Sentinel

**Cognitive Compliance Monitor — Human Oversight Intelligence for AI-Assisted Healthcare Workflows**

Sentinel sits inside AI-assisted prior authorization workflows and measures whether the humans using those tools are actually thinking — or just clicking through.

---

## The Problem

Every prior authorization workflow has the same invisible moment: the AI has spoken, and a human must now do something. Nobody is watching what that something is.

Your throughput metrics look fine. Your dashboard is green. But you have no idea how many of those decisions involved a human who was actually thinking — and how many involved a human who just clicked Accept.

## What Sentinel Measures

Sentinel tracks five behavioral metrics derived from observable, logged behavior — not surveys or self-reports:

| Metric | What It Captures | Flag Condition |
|--------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Time-to-Decision** | Elapsed time between AI recommendation and action | < 40 seconds + no notes |
| **Documentation Behavior** | Whether staff added notes, modified recs, or annotated decisions | Zero added notes on accepted rec |
| **Override Rate by Case Type** | How often humans disagree with AI, broken down by category | Override rate < 2% on any case type |
| **Escalation Behavior** | Whether complex cases are escalated for physician review | No escalation on ambiguous case types |
| **Verification Activity** | Whether clinical records were opened before acting | No record access before acceptance |

## The Engagement

This is a six-week service engagement, not a software deployment.

- **Weeks 1–2:** Scoping & Instrumentation
- **Weeks 3–5:** Active Measurement
- **Week 6:** Report & Readout

One workflow. One team. One baseline.

## The Deliverable

A structured baseline report delivered to compliance, legal, and operations leadership — with a number at the top that your leadership will quote for the next two years.
